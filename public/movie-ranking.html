The Best Movies According to Aya

My top movies of all-time

The Lake House

A lonely doctor who once occupied an unusual lakeside house begins to exchange love letters with its former resident, a frustrated architect.

One Day

One Day follows a couple on their life's journey on one particular date in their lives over a period of 22 years.

The Green Mile

The story follows senior prison officer Paul Edgecomb, who is in charge of "The Green Mile," the death row wing of the prison.
